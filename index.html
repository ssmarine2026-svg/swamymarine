Company profile

Marine support built for speed, accountability, and documentation.

Swamy Marine Services (OPC) Private Limited is an India-based marine services company focused on container survey and inspection support. The company helps clients verify equipment condition, record visible damage, classify suitability, and maintain survey documentation for operational and commercial decisions.

Every assignment is approached with a practical surveyor mindset: inspect the box, document the condition, highlight risks, and deliver a report that teams can use without ambiguity.

Services detail

Survey services for every container handover point.

From leasing checks to return inspections, the service scope is designed to reduce disputes, support claims, and keep cargo decisions grounded in visible evidence.

01

On-Hire Container Survey

Pre-lease inspection to record existing dents, repairs, door condition, floor quality, CSC plate visibility, cleanliness, and cargo suitability before the container is accepted.

02

Off-Hire Survey

Return-stage assessment that compares container condition, identifies new damage, documents repair needs, and supports fair settlement between lessor and lessee.

03

Condition & Damage Report

Detailed visual reporting for side walls, roof, undercarriage, doors, locking gear, flooring, front panel, corrosion, deformation, contamination, and structural concerns.

04

Cargo & Depot Support

Inspection assistance during loading, unloading, sealing, and depot movements with clear photographic evidence for operational teams and documentation desks.

Inspection method

A structured checklist from outside frame to inside floor.

  • Exterior, side wall, roof, front wall, and undercarriage checks
  • Door gaskets, locking bars, hinges, handles, and seal condition
  • Interior floor, cleanliness, odor, light leaks, and water tightness
  • Photo-backed observations with practical repair recommendations
